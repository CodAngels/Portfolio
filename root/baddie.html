Baddie

Brief Description

This is a Harvard based gifting platform that enables you to shop and gift your friends during the times you want them to feel you're there for them!
Be it the midterm season, finals season, reading period, their birthdays, and any other day!


Just add the items to the cart, add the address, add a custom note and leave the rest to the Baddie team to deliver your item.


Every day is a Gift Day!
