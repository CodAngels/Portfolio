BuyBye

Brief Description

Every Spring semester, seniors spam college emails with numerous powerpoints of things they plan to sell and the prices they charge. However, these powerpoints are just sent to the house's emailing list and there is no central way to manage the sold items, the offers received and the sold items.

BuyBye is a centralised platform that makes it easy to connect seniors selling their items with others students willing to buy the items. The items are no longer restricted to a single house but the whole school. It also utilizes a bid system where a seller can receive multiple bids for the same item. It also tracks what bids have been accepted and delivered.

No more powerpoints, no more spamming,
Just snap a picture, a short description and get bids!
