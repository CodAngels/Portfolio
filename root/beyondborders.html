BeyondBorders

Brief Description

Moving to a new state in the US to settle down is a hectic and time consuming process, beginning with making sure the state you choose matches your preferences, ideologies and has favorable economy to actually packing up and going there. But what if this stressful decision process was simplified to reduce the stress while considering multiple data sources to ensure you make the right decision.

BeyondBorders is a d3 backed web application that crunches the data from official government state statistics sources and visualizes them using easy-to-comprehend diagrams and data tools. It factors in data line average level of income, top employers, crime rates, state income taxes and policies like gun laws. It then matches you to a state that fits your preferences and lets you explore and compare it with other states.
