MeanIt

Brief Description

This is a mobile accountability app that allows you to make plans with your friends and ensures you both follow through with
the plans by depositing an equal amount to a central account. If both of you follow through with the plans, the amount goes into
the food/beverage expenses of your plans.


However, if one of the parties cancels without a 24hr notice, they forfeit the amount they deposited as an inconvenience fee
for the other party with the service taking a percentage cut.


Make plans and don't get disappointed or get a consolation fee for the inconvenience!


Make a plan and Mean It!
