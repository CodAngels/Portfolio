Optimus

Brief Description

Who doesn't love confettis when they complete a task? Who doesn't look forward to ticking a task off their todo-list?
Optimus is a productivity tool that allows you to not only add daily tasks to your to-do list but motivate you to begin them early,
finish them sooner and track your productivity.


You can also share with your friends to see who harvests the most out of their day!

This is all the validation you need! Getting things done and having fun without it being OXYMORONIC!
