wishList

Brief Description

It is your friend's birthday; it is your anniversary; it is colleague's wedding Whatever the ceremony, you always want to gift the perfect gift; a balance between something meaningful and something they really want. You also don't want to gift something someone else already got them The worst response to a gift is, "Another electric kettle!"


That is where wishlist helps you out. Wishlist is a chrome extension that allows you to add links/urls of the gifts you stumble upon and would like a friend to gift you. Once connected, your friends can see the items in an ordered list with those already purchased marked off If you are invited to a friend's wishlist, you can see gifts they'd like and the links to the places they could be found. Therefore, you can easily purchasethe gifts just from the wishlist.


In cases where you are really late and the wishlist has already been fullfilled, you could use our customGift tool to get the perfect gift for your friend by filling out a simple form and leaving the rest to the our algorithm that leverages AI to recommend the perfectly curated gift to match your friend's tastes.


Gifting has never been easier!
